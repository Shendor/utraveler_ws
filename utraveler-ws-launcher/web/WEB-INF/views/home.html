Main Features

uTraveler helps you to organize, plan, record and save your trips or other events, share your best moments with your friends in Facebook.

Manage your trips offline

Wherever you travel you can add some photos or just post some messages and when you have access to the internet - upload it to the Cloud

Plan your route

Create some routes using Google Maps, export to KML\KMZ files and add them to your trip.

Track your spending

Record how much money you spent for a particular thing during your trip

Facebook integration

With uTraveler you can share your photos or post some messages to Facebook, write and see comments and likes of a post.

User profile customization

Customize your profile changing avatar, cover photo, name and description.

Interface customization

Customize the interface changing the colors of background, foreground or text.
